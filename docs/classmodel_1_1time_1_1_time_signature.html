Detailed Description

TimeSignatures are exactly what they sound like in the Western music theory sense. These are useful as they define how RhythmTrees divide on the first few levels (3/4 versus 6/8) and will eventually be useful in determining how transitions between TimeSignatures work.

events.sounding.Note: These are in many ways structurally similar to Counts. The major difference is that there's no reason to ever reduce a time.TimeSig from say, 4/4 to 2/2 to 1/1.
